INGREDIENTS

These are the ingredients used in making the yam and egg sauce dish.

  • Yam tuber
  • Eggs
  • Onions
  • Peppers
  • Tomatoes
  • salt
  • knorr/maggi
  • Groundnut oil

STEPS

This is the step by step process of making this dish.

  1. Get a pot, fill it halfway with water, add a pinch of salt and put it on a stove to boil.
  2. Cut your tuber of yam into pieces depending on how much you can eat and peel the back off.
  3. Put your cut yam into the pot of boiling water and let the yam boil until it becomes soft enough for consumption.
  4. While letting the yam boil chop up your onions, peppers and tomatoes.
  5. Break your eggs in a bowl and add a pinch of salt, some maggi/knorr and whisk it all together.
  6. Get a pan and add some groundnut oil(about 2 to 3 teaspoons). Put this pan of oil on a stove to heat it up
  7. Now and onions, tomatoes and peppers to the pan of hot oil and let them all fry together to form a sauce.
  8. You can decide to add extra salt and knorr/maggi to the sauce to give it more flavour.
  9. Add your whisked eggs to the sauce in the pan to fry it and get your egg sauce.
  10. After boiling your yam, use a filter to drain the water out leaving the boiled yam behind.
  11. Serve the yam on a plate and the egg sauce at the side. Your dish is ready!
